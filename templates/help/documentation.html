PDF Upload & Parsing

Our parser automatically extracts information from your resume and structures it for editing and optimization.

Best Practices

  • Text-based PDFs only — avoid scanned images or photos
  • Standard headings — use "Experience", "Education", "Skills"
  • Simple layouts — avoid complex multi-column tables
  • File size — keep under 10 MB

AI-Powered Resume Optimization

What Gets Optimized

Keywords

Missing keywords from the job description are naturally inserted into your resume.

Action Verbs

Weak verbs replaced with strong ones like "achieved", "led", "implemented".

Quantification

Suggestions for adding metrics and numbers to make achievements more impactful.

Formatting

Section headings and dates standardized to be ATS-friendly.

How to Use

  1. Navigate to your resume and click Fix My Resume
  2. Paste the job description you're applying for
  3. Review the side-by-side comparison
  4. Accept or reject individual changes
  5. Save the optimized version
Your Original is Safe

Optimization creates a new version. Your original resume is never modified.

Version Control

Every change automatically creates a new version — you never lose your work.

  • Track how your resume evolves over time
  • Restore any previous version non-destructively
  • Compare two versions side-by-side

Version Comparison Colors

  • Green — content that was added
  • Red — content that was removed
  • Amber — content that was modified
Pro Tip

Use version comparison to understand which changes improved your ATS score the most.
